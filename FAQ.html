FAQs

What is INN about?

INN is a comprehensive online platform that provides the latest and most relevant news across various categories, including but not limited to politics, business, technology, entertainment, and sports.

How to login in News Portal?

You can create account for login in INN or you can connect your Google account to login in INN.

Can I subscribe to receive updates?

Yes, you can subscribe to our newsletter to receive regular updates directly in your inbox. Look for the subscription option on the homepage or in the menu.

Are the news articles on INN reliable?

We prioritize accuracy and reliability in our news reporting. Our team of experienced journalists follows ethical standards to ensure that the information provided is trustworthy and well-sourced.

How can I contact the editorial team?

If you have any questions, feedback, or concerns, you can reach out to our editorial team through the "Contact Us" page. We value your input and aim to address your inquiries promptly.
